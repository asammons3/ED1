Teardown Investigation:

For the Teardown Invenstigation, I chose a remote that controls a remote controlled car. The purpose of this project was to dismantle an electronic device so that we could understand each of the parts and how they worked together to make the object function.

Teardown of Remote Control

  1. This protected the batteries. The batteries are an important part of this object, because they provide the circuit board with energy to run.

  1. The front shell was the case that kept all of the parts in one place, and protected the circuit board from being damaged

  1. These control the car’s movement. One of them makes the car move forward and backwards, and the other moves it right and left. These are important to make the car move.

  1. The circuit board is the most important aspect. This connects the remote control with the car so that signals can be passed back and forth. These signals are then sent to the car through a small opening in the shell.
